Group Processes and Communications


As a group, we were all able to contribute a substantial amount of work and communication together through platforms such as team meetings and a group chat on Facebook messenger as we all understand that not all of us are active on teams in contrast to Facebook messenger. Through messenger, we are able to communicate efficiently at least three to four times a week and can update each other in terms of our progression throughout our group assignment and schedule a meeting in our spare time on Microsoft Teams. In terms of a group members not responding to communications, we as a team will reach out to that member and check in on them as to why they were unable to join a meeting. However, in this case, all group members are always participating in our meetings, and we have not yet had to check in on a member as everyone turns up to the meeting. If one of us were unable to attend a meeting or will be late, we always try to update the team on why we could not attend or how long we would be late for.
